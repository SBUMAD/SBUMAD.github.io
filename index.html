What is SBUMAD?

Official blog for SBUMAD General Body Meetings and Tutorials

SBUMAD is a speicial interest group(SIG) of Stony Brook University's larger Computing Soceity. The Computing Society is the campus's offical Association of Computing Machinery chapter. Click here for more info

The goal of the Mobile App Developers is to encourage Stony Brook students to develop for Mobile platforms as well as pursue entrepreneurial ambitious by collaborating with fellow passionate students

Meetings are held every Friday at 4:30pm until about 6:30pm in room CSE2311
